POP-UP FAQ

Why make a reservation instead of just walk-in?

Breizh Original Crêperie is a small space, and we want to create an intimate environment for our guests. Making a reservation allows us to anticipate who is coming and to give guests an all-encompassing experience while being prepared.

How do I make a reservation?

Click on the red bold link above that says "click here" or email the reservations department with the following information: 1. Name and contact number 2. Number of guests 3. Date and time preferred 4. Any allergies or dietary restrictions to note.

Do you take walk-ins?

Yes. Please note that we are a cash only premises.

Are children welcome?

Yes definitely!

What days are you open?

Every Saturday and Sunday for dinner service 6-9pm.

What are the payment methods at the Restaurant?

Only cash is accepted at the premises. No split bills please. There are atms readily available just a short walk across on Albany Highway i.e. NAB, Commonwealth, etc. We apologize if any inconvenience caused.

Will you accommodate allergies or special dietary restrictions?

If you have any specific or serious allergy please include that in your bookings form 48 hours prior to the dinner. We will be able to accommodate vegetarians however no other dietary restrictions can be accommodated at this time.

What if I am late for my reservation?

It is our intention that all our guests experience the full menu and dining experience that we have taken much care to create. We would request that our guests arrive on time to ensure smooth service throughout the evening.

Can I take photos in the restaurant?

Photography is welcome but we would ask that you refrain from using flash photography to preserve the intimacy of the space for all our quests.

Where do I park?

There is some free parking behind the restaurant. Alternatively street parking is free for the first 30 minutes and then parking ticket applies. Street parking is free from 8pm onwards.

Is Breizh Original Crêperie available for private parties?

Yes, if you are interested in buying out the restaurant for the evening please email reservations department.
