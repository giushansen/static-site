Menu Recommendations

1. One galette and one crêpe are included in a meal ticket for the Pop-up Event that you purchase.

2. Strictly BYO - We recommend especially French Cider (Dry or Semi-dry) to pair with your meal. Recommended Ciders also include: Rekorderlig Premium Apple Cider; Dirty Granny Apple Cider; James Squire Orchard Crush.

3. Water is included in your ticket, however below is a list of beverages you may choose from at the Restaurant. A bill will be presented at the end of your meal for any beverages and extra meals purchased.

4. Please note that only cash accepted at premises. Credit card/ eftpos payment methods is under construction.

Our specialty savoury Crêpes are made of our Organic buckwheat flour. Although Buckwheat is gluten-free as an ingredient, we cannot guarantee that your galette may not be contaminated by any gluten during the cooking process.
