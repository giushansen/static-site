About Us

Top-Grade French Crepes Meet Culinary-Ready Perth!

Using the tastiest of ingredients, cooking each Galette and Crepe to order and served with a bolee of Cider...

Is what Western Australia's first pop-up Creperie is doing. Breizh Original Creperie is not an eaterie in the typical sense yet rather, it is part of the Australian wave of restaurants who care about the quality and ethic of food. It is a concept rather than a physical location. We serve galettes and crepes in some very unique and cool spaces around town for a small group of people every Sunday night.

Breizh Original is a collaboration between 2 like-minded and creative French brothers, professionally-taught Crepier Guillaume and Hospitality-expert Adrien. Every week the Breizh Original crepiers scour the Farmer's Markets in an effort to bring you the very best that WA has to offer. With an ever changing seasonal menu, intimate setting, and carefully crafted experience, Breizh Original promises to be the most unique and delicious Bretagne dinner you can find in Australia!
