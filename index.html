POP-UP FAQ

Why purchase a ticket instead of just taking reservations?

Breizh Original Crêperie is a small space, and we want to create an intimate environment for our guests. Ticketing allows us to anticipate who is coming and to give guests an all-encompassing experience while being prepared.

How do I purchase a ticket for the pop-up event?

Step 1. Click on the link above "Purchase ticket here" and it will direct you to a form. Complete it with the information required accordingly and submit.

Step 2. You will receive an email by our reservations department within 24 hours to confirm your seating, with a link to make payment of your tickets.

Step 3. Click on the link provided in our email to make payment of the ticket. You have up to 24 hours to make full payment. Once payment is received then the meal ticket will be sent to you directly via the email you have provided.

Are children welcome?

Please be aware that it is a commercial open kitchen. There are hazards such as kitchen staff cooking openly and hot cookingware are accessible. It is recommended by the venue manager that only guests 12 years of age and above attend.

What days are you open?

Friday evenings the 8th and 15th August this month for dinner service. Further events will be promoted soon.

How much does a ticket to Breizh Original cost?

The price will vary based on the ever changing seasonal menu.

What are the payment methods at the Restaurant?

Only cash is accepted at the premises. Apologies for any inconvenience as we are in the process of setting up a credit card/ eftpos systems.

What does my ticket include? What if I want more food?

There will be two menus to choose between which include an appetizer, salad side, main galette, and dessert crêpes. There will be a limited option to purchase extra dishes during the event. A bill will be presented at the end of the meal for beverages or extra meals purchased. Water will be complimentary. Strictly BYO.

Can I get a refund once purchased? Can I exchange my ticket for a different night?

Unfortunately no, just as attending a sport event, concert or the theatre, all sales are final. We cannot cancel your ticket or reschedule for another night. If you cannot come, we recommend that you transfer your tickets to someone else.

Will you accommodate allergies or special dietary restrictions?

If you have any specific or serious allergy please include that in your bookings form 48 hours prior to the dinner. We will be able to accommodate vegetarians however no other dietary restrictions can be accommodated at this time.

What if I am late for my reservation?

It is our intention that all our guests experience the full menu and dining experience that we have taken much care to create. We would request that our guests arrive on time to ensure smooth service throughout the evening.

Do you take walk-ins?

Unfortunately, due to the size of the venue we will not be able to reserve a table for walk-ins.

Can I take photos in the restaurant?

Photography is welcome but we would ask that you refrain from using flash photography to preserve the intimacy of the space for all our quests.

Where do I park?

There is some free parking along Court Street after 5pm. Alternatively use Lord Street or Lincoln Street - there may be a charge to park in this area.

Is Breizh Original Crêperie available for private parties?

Yes, if you are interested in buying out the restaurant for the evening please email booking@breizhoriginal.com.au.
